Studio — Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 7, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how Studio ("Studio", the "Service", "we", "us") collects, uses, stores, and discloses information when you use the Studio application. Studio is a content management application that helps social media creators publish videos to TikTok, YouTube, Instagram, and Facebook from a single interface.

1. Information Studio Collects

Studio collects only the information needed to operate its features:

CategoryExamplesPurpose
Account infoEmail address (when you sign in)To create your Studio account and sync your data across devices
Content dataVideo files, thumbnails, descriptions, hashtags, ideas, references, linksTo plan and publish content to platforms you select
OAuth tokensAccess & refresh tokens for connected platforms (TikTok, YouTube, Meta)To publish on your behalf via the official APIs of the platforms you connect
Connected-account metadataConnected account usernames, IDs, profile picture URLsTo display which accounts are linked in the Profile page
Public engagement metricsAggregated views, likes, comments returned by platform APIsTo display performance dashboards in the Stats page
Technical logsAPI request timestamps, error messagesDebugging and reliability of Studio

Studio does not collect: precise geolocation, contacts, browsing history outside the application, biometric data, or payment information. Studio does not place advertising cookies and does not track you across other websites.

2. How Studio Uses Your Information

  • To allow you to publish your videos to the platforms you have authorized;
  • To present aggregated public engagement metrics returned by those platforms;
  • To generate optional AI-assisted post descriptions when you request, via the Anthropic Claude API;
  • To maintain the security, reliability, and integrity of Studio.

Studio does not sell, rent, or trade your personal information. Studio does not use your content to train any machine-learning model.

3. Where Your Data Is Stored

Studio stores data in the following providers:

  • Supabase (managed PostgreSQL) — Studio account email, ideas, post metadata, OAuth tokens, analytics snapshots. Tokens are encrypted at rest.
  • Vercel Blob — temporary storage for uploaded video files (only as long as needed to forward them to the destination platform; deleted afterwards or within 24 hours).
  • Vercel — application hosting and serverless function execution.
  • Browser local storage — for offline drafts on your device only; never transmitted unless you choose to sync.

OAuth access tokens are used only to call the official APIs of the platforms you have connected. Tokens are never shared with any other third party.

4. Third-Party Services

When you connect a third-party platform to Studio, your interactions with that platform are also governed by that platform's privacy policy:

  • TikTok: tiktok.com/legal/privacy-policy
  • YouTube / Google: policies.google.com/privacy — Studio's use and transfer of information received from Google APIs adheres to the Google API Services User Data Policy, including the Limited Use requirements.
  • Meta (Facebook, Instagram): facebook.com/privacy/policy
  • Anthropic: anthropic.com/legal/privacy
  • Supabase: supabase.com/privacy
  • Vercel: vercel.com/legal/privacy-policy

5. Data Retention

Content data and analytics are retained for as long as you use Studio. OAuth tokens are retained until you disconnect the corresponding platform from the Profile page or revoke access through that platform's own settings. You may delete all of your data at any time using the "Reset all data" option in Studio. Account deletion is irreversible.

6. Your Rights

Depending on your jurisdiction (GDPR, CCPA, etc.) you may have the right to:

  • Access the personal data Studio holds about you;
  • Correct inaccurate data;
  • Delete your data ("right to be forgotten");
  • Withdraw consent and disconnect linked platforms;
  • Object to or restrict certain processing;
  • Receive your data in a portable format.

To exercise any of these rights, email the address at the bottom of this page. Studio aims to respond within thirty (30) days.

7. Children's Privacy

Studio is not directed to anyone under the age of 13 (or the equivalent minimum age in your jurisdiction). Studio does not knowingly collect personal data from children. If we learn that a child has provided us with personal data, we will delete it.

8. Security

Studio uses HTTPS for all communications, encrypted token storage in the database, and least-privilege OAuth scopes when requesting platform permissions. However, no method of electronic storage or transmission is 100% secure. Use Studio at your own risk.

9. International Data Transfers

Studio's infrastructure providers (Supabase, Vercel) operate data centres in multiple regions including the United States and the European Union. By using Studio you consent to your data being transferred to and processed in those regions in accordance with their respective privacy frameworks.

10. Changes to This Policy

Studio may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Material changes will be reflected by the "Last updated" date above. Continued use of Studio after changes indicates acceptance.

11. Contact

For privacy questions, data requests, or concerns about Studio, contact: wittyvisionary@yahoo.com.
